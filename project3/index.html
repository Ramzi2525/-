Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Fixora.

How do you verify service providers?

Every provider goes through a 5-step verification process: ID verification, background check, skill assessment, reference checks, and ongoing customer review monitoring. Only 23% of applicants pass our verification.

What is your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 24 hours before the service. Within 24 hours, a small fee may apply. Emergency cancellations due to provider issues are always free.

Do you offer same-day service?

Yes! 85% of services offer same-day booking. Emergency services like plumbing and electrical often have 2-hour response times.

How does payment work?

No upfront payment required. You only pay after the service is completed to your satisfaction. We accept credit cards, digital wallets, and bank transfers.

What's included in the satisfaction guarantee?

If you're not 100% satisfied, we'll either refund your payment or send another professional to fix the issue at no extra cost.

Can I book recurring services?

Absolutely! Set up weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly recurring bookings. You'll get the same provider each time and discounted rates for recurring services.
